OUR ORIGIN STORY

We're not criminals. We just play them on merch. Here's how it all went wrong — in the best possible way.

🕵️ Case #001

HOW IT ALL STARTED

It was a dark and stormy night — well, it was actually a Tuesday afternoon — when our founder Sarah was watching her fourth consecutive episode of Forensic Files in a hoodie that had definitely seen better days. Her roommate walked in and said, "You need to get a life." Sarah said, "I need better merch."

And thus, Crime Shoppers was born. What started as a personal obsession with true crime podcasts, murder mystery novels, and making everyone around her vaguely uncomfortable at dinner parties grew into a full-blown merchandise empire.

We've never solved a crime. We've never been accused of one either (for the record). But we have created some truly excellent apparel and accessories for people who spend more time researching cold cases than their own retirement accounts.

🔍
"I wanted merch that captured the essence of spending your Saturday researching a case from 1987 while eating cereal directly from the box. Crime Shoppers is that merch."
— Sarah M., Founder & Chief Evidence Officer
🎯 The Mission

WE'RE NOT CRIMINALS

We just play them on merch.

Crime Shoppers exists for the millions of people who spend their evenings listening to true crime podcasts, watching documentary series about cold cases, and explaining to their significant others why yes, they do need to know the details of a 1972 kidnapping in rural Ohio.

We believe that dark humor is a valid coping mechanism, that "murderino" is a perfectly acceptable identity, and that wearing a "Serial Chiller" t-shirt to brunch is a form of self-expression, not a red flag.
