🛍️ Shopify

SHOPIFY INTEGRATION

The gold standard for e-commerce. Connect Shopify Buy Buttons or build a full storefront.

🔘

Buy Button Channel

Embed Shopify's Buy Button directly into Crime Shoppers pages. Your inventory lives in Shopify; the button shows up on your site.

  1. 1Create a Shopify store at shopify.com
  2. 2Add your 12 products with variants (size, color)
  3. 3Go to Sales Channels → Buy Button in admin
  4. 4Create a Buy Button for each product or a collection
  5. 5Customize colors to match (#FFD700 primary, #0d0d0d background)
  6. 6Copy the embed code and paste into your HTML
  7. 7Replace placeholder product cards with embed divs
💡

Pro Tip: Use the Storefront API token (not the Admin API key) in your buy button embed. Keep tokens server-side in production.

Cost: Free Buy Button channel (requires Shopify plan from $29/mo)

💻

Embed Code Structure

Here's the structure of a Shopify Buy Button embed. Replace the placeholder values with your actual Shopify credentials.


<div id="product-component-1234567"></div>


<script>
  var client = ShopifyBuy.buildClient({
    domain: 'your-store.myshopify.com',
    storefrontAccessToken: 'YOUR_TOKEN_HERE'
  });
  ShopifyBuy.UI.onReady(client).then(function(ui) {
    ui.createComponent('product', {
      id: 'YOUR_PRODUCT_ID',
      node: document.getElementById('product-component-1234567'),
      options: { /* customize styles here */ }
    });
  });
</script>
⚠️

Security Note: The Storefront Access Token is safe to expose in client-side JavaScript. Never embed your Admin API key.

Full Shopify Storefront

Migrate completely to Shopify for the most powerful checkout, abandoned cart recovery, and analytics.

  1. 1Create your Shopify store and add products
  2. 2Install a dark theme (Dawn with customizations, or purchase a premium dark theme)
  3. 3Connect Printful/Printify via their Shopify app for fulfillment
  4. 4Set up your custom domain (crimeshoppers.com)
  5. 5Configure tax and shipping settings
  6. 6Add Shopify Payments or Stripe for checkout
  7. 7Point your domain's DNS to Shopify servers

Cost: Basic $29/mo • Shopify $79/mo • Advanced $299/mo

📦 Amazon

AMAZON INTEGRATION

Two powerful ways to leverage the world's largest retailer: Merch on Demand and the Affiliate Program.

📦

Merch on Demand

Amazon prints, ships, and handles customer service. You just upload designs and collect royalties. The laziest path to passive income.

  1. 1Apply at merch.amazon.com (waitlist applies)
  2. 2Once approved, upload your designs as PNG (300 DPI, transparent background)
  3. 3Choose product types: T-shirts, hoodies, phone cases, etc.
  4. 4Set your royalty price (Amazon sets the base cost)
  5. 5Write SEO-optimized titles and bullets (include "true crime", "murderino", etc.)
  6. 6Amazon lists and sells the products — you get paid monthly
💰

Royalty example: A $19.99 T-shirt might earn you ~$3.50–$5.00 per sale. Low per-unit, but scales massively with Amazon's traffic.

🔗

Amazon Associates (Affiliates)

Link to Amazon products (true crime books, documentaries, accessories) and earn 1–10% commission on every sale referred from your site.

  1. 1Sign up at affiliate-program.amazon.com
  2. 2Get your unique affiliate tracking ID
  3. 3Add ?tag=YOURID-20 to any Amazon product URL
  4. 4Add affiliate links to blog posts, product descriptions, newsletters
  5. 5Include the required disclosure: "As an Amazon Associate I earn from qualifying purchases"

🖨️ Printful

PRINTFUL INTEGRATION

The most popular print-on-demand fulfillment service. High quality, global warehouses, and a solid API.

🖨️

Printful + Shopify

The most recommended combo. Printful handles all production and shipping automatically when an order comes through Shopify.

  1. 1Create a free account at printful.com
  2. 2Connect your Shopify store via the Printful Shopify app
  3. 3Create products in Printful (T-shirts, hoodies, mugs, etc.)
  4. 4Upload your Crime Shoppers designs (min. 150 DPI for apparel)
  5. 5Sync products to Shopify — they appear as regular products
  6. 6When a customer orders, Printful auto-fulfills and ships
  7. 7You're charged production cost; you keep the markup
📐

Design specs: T-shirts — 4500 x 5400px PNG. Mugs — 2700 x 1350px. All designs should be 300 DPI with transparent background.

⚡

Printful API

For advanced integrations, use Printful's REST API to automate order creation, product sync, and fulfillment status updates.

// Printful API — Create Order (Node.js example)
const response = await fetch(
  'https://api.printful.com/orders',
  {
    method: 'POST',
    headers: {
      'Authorization': `Bearer ${PRINTFUL_KEY}`,
      'Content-Type': 'application/json'
    },
    body: JSON.stringify({
      recipient: { name, address1, city, country_code },
      items: [{ sync_variant_id, quantity }]
    })
  }
);
  1. 1Get API key from Printful Dashboard → Settings → API
  2. 2Store key securely in environment variables (never expose client-side)
  3. 3Use GET /products to fetch your catalog
  4. 4Use POST /orders to submit orders for fulfillment

🎨 Printify

PRINTIFY INTEGRATION

A network of 90+ print providers worldwide. More options, competitive pricing, and a powerful API.

🎨

Printify Setup

Printify connects to Shopify, Etsy, WooCommerce, and more. Choose from 90+ print providers for the best price/quality combination.

  1. 1Sign up free at printify.com
  2. 2Connect your store (Shopify, Etsy, WooCommerce, etc.)
  3. 3Browse the product catalog — pick items for each of your 12 SKUs
  4. 4For each product, choose a print provider (compare price, location, reviews)
  5. 5Upload your designs using the Printify mockup generator
  6. 6Set retail prices and publish to your store
  7. 7Orders auto-route to your chosen print provider
💡

Cost advantage: Printify often undercuts Printful on base prices by 20–30%, especially if you use providers in the US for US orders and EU for EU orders.

🔌

Printify API

Automate product creation, order management, and shipping updates using Printify's REST API.

// Printify API — Get Products
const res = await fetch(
  'https://api.printify.com/v1/shops/{shop_id}/products.json',
  {
    headers: {
      'Authorization': `Bearer ${PRINTIFY_TOKEN}`
    }
  }
);
const products = await res.json();

// Webhook for order status updates:
// POST https://api.printify.com/v1/shops/{id}/webhooks
🔔

Webhooks: Register webhooks to get real-time notifications when orders are fulfilled, so you can update customers automatically.

🎪 Artist Marketplaces

REDBUBBLE & TEEPUBLIC

No store setup needed. Upload designs, earn royalties. Perfect for getting started with zero overhead.

🔴

Redbubble

The largest artist marketplace. Upload once, sell on 70+ products including T-shirts, stickers, phone cases, pillows, and more. Perfect for the Crime Shoppers product lineup.

  1. 1Create an artist account at redbubble.com
  2. 2Upload your designs as PNG/TIFF, min. 2400 x 3200px
  3. 3Tag your work with: "true crime", "murderino", "forensic files", "crime podcast", "serial killer", etc.
  4. 4Enable all product types that fit your designs
  5. 5Set your markup percentage (default 20%, can adjust per product)
  6. 6Share your Redbubble profile link from your Crime Shoppers site
🏷️

SEO matters: Redbubble is heavily search-driven. Use specific tags like "SSDGM", "murderino", "true crime junkie", "forensic files fan" for discoverability.

Start Selling on Redbubble →
👕

TeePublic

Owned by Redbubble but operates separately. Known for frequent sales and a strong T-shirt and apparel focus. Great for the Crime Shoppers clothing lineup.

  1. 1Create a designer account at teepublic.com
  2. 2Upload designs — same files as Redbubble work here
  3. 3TeePublic frequently runs sitewide sales (designs sell at $14–$20)
  4. 4Add tags and choose design categories (Humor, Dark Humor, Crime)
  5. 5Promote your TeePublic store URL on social media and your site
💰

Earnings: Standard T-shirts earn ~$4 at base price, more during regular-priced windows. Sitewide sales reduce your cut but massively increase volume.

Recommended Strategy

For Crime Shoppers, we recommend a multi-channel approach:

  1. 1Primary: Shopify + Printful for full brand control and highest margins
  2. 2Passive: Amazon Merch on Demand for marketplace reach
  3. 3Discovery: Redbubble + TeePublic for organic discovery at zero cost
  4. 4Affiliate: Amazon Associates for recommended true crime books/content
🎯

The goal: Use Redbubble/TeePublic to build audience, then funnel fans to your Shopify store for exclusive products and better margins.
